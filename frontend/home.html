Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about IntelliCheck

Q1 How secure is my students' data? ▾
All uploads are encrypted in transit and processed using privacy-first defaults. Retention is configurable in your settings.
Q2 What sources are checked? ▾
IntelliCheck compares submissions against web sources, academic publications, and previously submitted student work to find matches and high-similarity passages.
Q3 Can it detect paraphrasing or AI-generated text? ▾
Yes — our analysis flags paraphrased sections and patterns typically found in AI-assisted writing, but human review is recommended for final decisions.
